Sonic Mega Logistics Services

We deliver everything — fast, safe, and reliable across Nigeria. Whether you're sending urgent packages or restocking your store, Sonic Mega is here to move it for you.

  • Delivery

    Everyday errands, right on time. From documents to small items, our real-time riders handle instant delivery across town.


  • Food

    Hot meals, delivered fresh. Partner with local restaurants or send homemade food to loved ones, all within minutes.


  • Grocery

    Daily essentials at your doorstep. Order fruits, vegetables, and household goods from trusted supermarkets in your area.


  • Gadget

    Secure tech delivery. Whether it’s phones, tablets, or accessories — we handle fragile tech with care.

  • Pharmacy

    Health deliveries you can count on. Send or receive prescription and over-the-counter medicine from certified pharmacies.


  • Flowers

    Deliver joy, instantly. From surprise bouquets to celebration arrangements, get flowers delivered same-day.


  • Baby

    Essentials for little ones. Send baby food, diapers, and toys quickly and safely to parents or caregivers.


  • Personal Care

    Wellness at your fingertips. Shampoos, lotions, and hygiene products — delivered quickly when you need them.


  • Packages

    Business or personal — we move it. From documents to boxes, schedule and track any delivery with ease.

    Run your business smarter. We power deliveries for online sellers and retail stores with seamless logistics
